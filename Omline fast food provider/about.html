About Us

Welcome to Qazi’s Teezy’s Pizza & Spicy Bro’s, where taste meets tradition! Founded in 2024 by Abdullah Mansoor Qazi, our journey began with a small setup and a big dream — to serve mouthwatering, high-quality food made with our secret masalas and a passion for perfection.

At Qazi’s Teezy’s Pizza & Spicy Bro’s, we believe great food brings people together. Our team is dedicated to providing fresh ingredients, unbeatable flavor, and exceptional service to every customer. From cheesy, sizzling pizzas to spicy, flavorful dishes, every bite tells a story of love and authenticity.

What started as a single outlet has now grown into a brand with branches in Lahore, Karachi, and Islamabad, along with international locations in Saudi Arabia, Qatar, and America. Our next big step is expanding to London, bringing our signature taste to a global audience.

Join us and experience the flavor revolution — only at Qazi’s Teezy’s Pizza & Spicy Bro’s, where every meal is made with heart.
